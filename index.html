About Me

I like to get crafty and do things the old way in my free time. By that I mean I push plain old vanilla JavaScript to it's limits. I think it's the best way to make the mosts of current libraries and frameworks.

Now that you know I love JavaScript, it's important you know that I love animation just as much. I started with keyframes and my latest interests are breaking down open source projects and seeing how they work.

I owe it to the generous people I've met along my learning path for allowing me and even encouraging me to break their stuff. A formal shout out to you all!
